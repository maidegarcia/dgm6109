About

Who am I?

I am an audiovisual artist and sound designer, passionate for the study and creation of sound in every aspect of film and video games. When I am not working, I am playing video games, recording sounds, going on hikes with my girlfriend or watching movies and tv shows for inspiration

My Experience

I have over three years of experience in production sound and two years of experience on postproduction audio, working as a recording engineer, sound editor and re-recording mixer. My work spans from short films and tv series to indie video games.
